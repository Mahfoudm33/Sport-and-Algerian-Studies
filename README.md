# Sport-and-Algerian-Studies
An interactive map with my publications of Sport, Culture and Society in Algeria
This is an interactive, map-based platform presenting research publications by
Prof. Mahfoud Amara** (Qatar University, College of Sport Sciences) on sport,
politics, identity, and society in Algeria — spanning colonial history to the present.

Visitors click on cities placed on a stylised map of Algeria to explore publications
linked to that location, including a summary, key takeaway, and thematic tags.
